campus life

blitzing

was established in 2015

What better way to showcase the multifaceted, talent buzzing buds than the Annual Day which we have fittingly christened Blitzing -A showcase of Talent! We take great pride in stating emphatically that every aspect of this extravaganza is in house. From the choreography of the dance to the costumes, from the selection of the themes and items to the compering, managing the stage are all planned and implemented in house. Additionally, the background videos are produced in our AV Centre and the invites are designed in house too. We outsource only the lighting, sound systems and the stage as the audience has multiplied multi-fold in the last few years. Electric energy and extreme confidence are the hallmarks of this event. The Cambridge principle of the teacher as facilitator comes into focus.Our students,under the able guidance and supervision of our endowed Faculty, run the entire show.



children's bazaar

was established in 2010

PTA's Children's Bazaar is a much awaited event each year. The students of USGS and PPSIJC transform into efficient entrepreneurs during this venture.The Children's Bazaar held close to the end of the year is about setting up stalls which are 'purchased' by groups of students and parents on a first come first serve basis. Choices and options for stall positions are higher in the preliminary round;parents and students excitedly go together. They then decide on eats, games and displays for their stalls and act as perfect salesman selling their wares luring prospective clients towards their stalls. The inherent purpose is to improve business skills; students build team spirit, learn how to run a new venture and understand the value of money. The students raise profits from the money invested, thus leading to a perfect cycle of investment (purchase), sale and earnings. A fun cum learning experience.
